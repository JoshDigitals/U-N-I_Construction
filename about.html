Our Journey

Company Timeline

From humble beginnings to industry leadership, here's how we've grown over the years.

2010

Company Founded

U N I Construction was established with a vision to provide quality construction services with integrity and professionalism.

2013

First Major Project

Completed our first major commercial project - a 25,000 sq ft office building that set new standards for quality in the region.

2016

Team Expansion

Grew our team to 50+ skilled professionals and expanded our capabilities to include civil engineering projects.

2019

Sustainability Focus

Launched our green building initiative and achieved LEED certification, demonstrating our commitment to sustainable construction.

2022

Technology Integration

Integrated advanced construction technologies including BIM, drone surveying, and project management software for enhanced efficiency.

2024

Industry Recognition

Received the Construction Excellence Award and expanded operations to serve clients across multiple states.
